Jun Ma - Projects

Control and optimization

  • Design of fixed-order controller for system with bounded parametric uncertainties considering the robust stability and robust performance in restricted frequency ranges.

  • Development of data-driven optimal control approach for controller design problems with structural constraints.

  • Developement of robust optimization approach in a parameter space to solve the H2 guaranteed cost control problem with parametric uncertainties.

  • Development of constrained optimization algorithms for a class of optimal control and robust control problems.

Precision mechatronics

  • Design and optimization of high-speed motion control systems, e.g., flexure-based biaxial gantry system, timing belt actuator for tray indexing, jerk-decoupling catridge system, etc.

Robotics

  • Development of neural network based approach for motion control of a planar snake robot.

Medical technology

  • Development of ultrasound-guided automated spinal landmark identification with a real-time needle insertion system for epidural anaesthesia procedures based on machine learning and image processing technique.
