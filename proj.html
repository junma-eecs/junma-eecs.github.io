Jun Ma - Projects

Control and Optimization

  • Development of optimization algorithms for optimal control and robust control problems, e.g., alternating direction method of multipliers (ADMM) algorithm, augmented Lagrangian method (ALM), cutting-plane optimization algorithm, gradient descent optimization algorithm, etc.

  • LMI characterizations for fixed-order robust controller synthesis considering the robust stability and robust performance in restricted frequency ranges.

  • Advanced data-driven methodologies for controller design problems.

  • Youla-Kucera-based parameterization and optimization for H2 guaranteed cost control and H-infinity guaranteed cost control.

  • Controller synthesis with structural constraints, e.g. decentralized control, sparse control, etc.

Mechatronics and Robotics

  • Design and control of high-speed and high-precision mechatronic systems, e.g., magnetically levitated nano-positioning system, flexure-based dual drive H-gantry stage, timing belt system for tray indexing, jerk-decoupling-cartridge-based linear direct feed drive, flexure-based electromagnetic linear actuator, etc.

  • Energy-oriented multi-objective optimization in sustainable advanced manufacturing.

  • Advanced motion control in robotics.

Intelligent Transportation System

  • Learning-inspired optimization for NP-hard problems in intelligent transportation systems.

Intelligent Healthcare

  • Image processing and machine learning for ultrasound-guided automated spinal landmark identification system for epidural anaesthesia procedures.
