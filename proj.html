Jun Ma - Projects

Intelligent transportation system

  • Currenly working on robust optimization and intellgent control of transportation systems.

Control and optimization

  • Development of optimization algorithms for optimal control and robust control problems, e.g. symmetric Gauss-Seidel alternating direction method of multipliers (ADMM) algorithm, symmetric Gauss-Seidel semi-proximal augmented Lagrangian method (ALM), cutting-plane optimization algorithm, gradient-based optimization algorithm, etc.

  • LMI characterizations for fixed-order robust controller synthesis considering the robust stability and robust performance in restricted frequency ranges.

  • Development of advanced data-driven approaches for controller design problems.

  • Youla-Kucera-based parameterizations for H2 guaranteed cost control and H-infinity guaranteed cost control.

  • Controller synthesis with structural constraints, e.g. decentralized control, sparse control, etc.

Mechatronics and robotics

  • Design and control of high-speed and high-precision mechatronic systems, e.g., flexure-based biaxial gantry stage, timing belt actuator, jerk-decoupling cartridge systme, magnetically levitation positioning stage, etc.

  • Motion control of a planar snake robot by neural network and sliding mode compensator.

Medical technology

  • Ultrasound-guided automated spinal landmark identification with a real-time needle insertion system for epidural anaesthesia procedures based on image processing and machine learning.
